Our promise

Our story behind Hatugy Bracelets represents more than just fashion. We're committed to showing our hispanic roots while adding an unique spin on what you wear on your wrist. Every Hatugy bracelet is precisely handcrafted by artisans from Dominican Republic and is sent straight to you.

Hatugy Bracelets' story begins with our move from Connecticut to North Carolina in 2011, where we met a change of pace from an urban area to a rural landscape. While adjusting from the bustling traffic of the city to the peaceful mountain ranges of North Carolina, we began to notice how charming, thriving, and united the community of Mount Airy is.

Our "pinky promise" involves giving you the ability to give back to the community of Mount Airy and other various community programs that Hatugy Bracelets support.
